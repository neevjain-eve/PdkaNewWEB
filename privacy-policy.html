1 Introduction

P. Dilip Kumar & Associates ("PDKA", "we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of visitors to this website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard any information you provide when you visit our website or contact us for professional services.

2 Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you contact us or use our website:

  • Name and contact details (email address, phone number)
  • Business or organisation name
  • Nature of service enquiry
  • Messages submitted through our contact form
  • Technical information such as browser type and IP address (collected automatically)

3 How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your professional enquiries
  • To provide and deliver professional services as agreed
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations under Indian law and ICAI guidelines
  • To improve the functionality and usability of this website

We do not use your information for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

4 Confidentiality of Client Information

All client information received in connection with professional engagements is treated as strictly confidential in accordance with the Code of Ethics of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI). Such information shall not be disclosed to any third party except where required by law or with the prior written consent of the client.

5 Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. Information may be shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Where required by a court of law, regulatory authority, or statutory body
  • With your explicit prior written consent
  • To professional advisors bound by confidentiality obligations

6 Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect information submitted to us from unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7 Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law. Client engagement records are retained in accordance with ICAI requirements and applicable statutory periods.

8 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information, subject to legal retention obligations
  • Withdraw consent for any discretionary use of your information

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at dilip@pdka.in.

9 Cookies

This website may use cookies to improve your browsing experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality of the website.
