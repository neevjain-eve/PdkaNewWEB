About the Firm

PDKA is a fast growing professional firm of Chartered Accountants committed to its values, quality policy and ICAI Standards. Over the last twenty-eight years of practice, the firm has created a niche for itself offering a bouquet of services to a selected clientele from India and other countries.

Based in Bangalore, the IT Capital of India, the firm has made a name for itself by offering end-to-end services under one roof — in particular for the IT & ITES industry and the Retail sector in India.

The firm serves clients across India, USA, Australia, Dubai, UK, Ireland, Singapore, Japan and Italy.
